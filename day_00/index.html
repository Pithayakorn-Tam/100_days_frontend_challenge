About Me

Hello, I’m Tam. I lived in Saraburi until I turned 18. I just graduated from Grade 12 in March this year. Now, I study at Thammasat University, where we learn to love people. I’m in the Faculty of Science, majoring in Computer Science. I’ve been studying this major for about five weeks, and I’ve already gained a lot of knowledge. Although I didn’t originally intend to choose this faculty and major, I don’t regret it. I see it as an opportunity to improve my skills more and more.

My Dream

My dream may not sound exciting. It might seem boring compared to other people’s dreams. Some dream of becoming the richest person in the world, a president, or a scientist. But my dream is simple: “I want to do what I truly want to do,” as long as it’s within the law. So, what do I want to do? I want to learn about things I’m curious about, play when I feel like it, and travel to places I want to visit—without worrying about money. It may sound boring, but to me, it’s the perfect life.
