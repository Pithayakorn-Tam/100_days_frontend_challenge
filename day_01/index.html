Wild rabbits are found across various continents, except Antarctica. They typically inhabit meadows, forests, grasslands, and even deserts. Their homesâ€”called warrensâ€”are complex networks of underground tunnels that provide shelter from predators and harsh weather. Rabbits are crepuscular, meaning they are most active during dawn and dusk. Their large eyes and ears are evolutionary adaptations that help them detect predators in low light. With nearly 360-degree vision and acute hearing, rabbits are constantly alert. Their powerful hind legs allow them to escape danger with swift, zigzagging hops.

ğŸ¡ Rabbit as a Pet

Pet rabbits can be affectionate, playful, and intelligent. They can be litter-trained and enjoy social interaction. However, they are also delicate and require gentle handling. Rabbits are best kept indoors or in secure outdoor enclosures to protect them from predators and extreme weather.

Key care tips:

  • Provide space to hop and explore.
  • Regular grooming, especially for long-haired breeds.
  • Annual vet checkups and vaccinations.
  • Enrichment toys to prevent boredom.
